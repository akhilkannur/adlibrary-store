Building a Scalable LinkedIn Ad Scraper

Published: January 28, 2026 • Reading time: 10 min

Most agencies struggle to find companies running LinkedIn ads in their niche. In this guide, I'll show you simple methods to identify prospects who are already spending money on LinkedIn advertising. You'll learn how to build targeted prospect lists from public ad data without needing technical skills.

Simple Methods to Find LinkedIn Advertisers

There are several ways to identify companies running LinkedIn ads without needing technical skills:

1. Manual Research Method

The simplest approach is to manually search LinkedIn's Ad Library:

  • Go to linkedin.com/ad-library
  • Enter industry keywords (e.g., "SaaS", "B2B software", "consulting")
  • Filter by geography to focus on your target market
  • Record companies with active ad campaigns

2. Systematic Search Approach

For better results, use a structured approach:

  • Create a list of industry keywords relevant to your niche
  • Search each keyword systematically
  • Record companies with 10+ active ads (shows serious ad spend)
  • Track ad formats being used (video, carousel, image)
  • Note which companies have been running ads consistently

3. Bulk Identification Strategies

Scale your prospect identification with these techniques:

  • Focus on specific industries where you have expertise
  • Look for companies with consistent ad presence (not just one-off campaigns)
  • Identify companies expanding their ad spend over time
  • Target companies using multiple ad formats (shows sophisticated approach)

What to Look For

When researching companies in the Ad Library, pay attention to:

  • Ad Volume: Companies with 20+ active ads likely have substantial budgets
  • Ad Duration: Ads running for weeks/months show commitment to the strategy
  • Creative Diversity: Multiple formats indicate sophisticated marketing
  • Geographic Scope: Expansion into new markets signals growth
  • Message Consistency: Refined messaging shows mature marketing approach

Turning Research Into Prospects

Once you've identified active advertisers, convert them to prospects:

  1. Qualify the companies: Ensure they fit your ideal customer profile
  2. Research decision makers: Find the right person to contact (CMO, Head of Marketing, etc.)
  3. Prepare your approach: Reference their specific ad campaigns in your outreach
  4. Time your outreach: Reach out when they're actively advertising (not during quiet periods)

Efficiency Tips

Maximize your research efficiency with these tips:

  • Spend 15-20 minutes per company to understand their ad strategy
  • Create a simple spreadsheet to track findings
  • Focus on 5-10 companies per day rather than rushing through dozens
  • Look for patterns across multiple companies in the same industry
  • Set up weekly searches to find new prospects consistently

By using these simple methods, you can build a pipeline of qualified prospects who are already investing in LinkedIn advertising and likely to be receptive to agency services.
